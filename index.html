1. Introduction and Scope

Welcome to the Privacy Policy of Speedo ("Company," "we," "us," or "our"). We operate a digital platform that facilitates gig-based connections between independent drivers and passengers (collectively, "Users"). Speedo is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, who we share it with, and the rights you have regarding your data.

By downloading, registering with, or using the Speedo mobile application and website (the "Platform"), you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. This policy applies to all users of our services in Sri Lanka and complies with applicable local data protection regulations.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide to Us

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when creating an account or using our services. This includes:

  • Identity Data: Name, phone number, email address, and profile photograph.
  • Verification Data (Drivers): Driver’s license details, national identity card (NIC) numbers, vehicle registration documents, insurance papers, and revenue license details required for vetting.
  • User Content: Reviews, ratings, feedback, and communications with customer support.

B. Information We Collect Automatically

When you use the Platform, we automatically collect certain technical data:

  • Location Data: To facilitate the "Choose Your Driver" feature, we collect precise or approximate location data from your mobile device. For drivers, this is collected when the app is running in the foreground or background to show availability to nearby passengers. For passengers, this is used to display the map of nearby gigs.
  • Device Information: We collect data about your mobile device, including hardware model, operating system, unique device identifiers, IP address, and mobile network information.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you interact with our services, including access times, app features used, and crash logs.

3. How We Use Your Information

Speedo uses the collected data for the following purposes:

  • Facilitating Services: To create and manage your account, verify your identity, and enable the manual selection of drivers by passengers based on location proximity.
  • Safety and Security: To vet drivers before they join the platform, monitor trip activity for safety, and prevent fraudulent activity.
  • Customer Support: To investigate and resolve your concerns, monitor and improve our customer support responses.
  • Communications: To send you service updates, security alerts, and administrative messages.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws in Sri Lanka, respond to legal requests, and protect our rights.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, we share data in the following circumstances:

  • Between Users: When a passenger selects a driver, we share the Passenger’s first name and pickup location with the Driver. We share the Driver’s name, photo, vehicle details, rating, and current location with the Passenger to facilitate the pickup.
  • Service Providers: We may share data with third-party vendors who provide services such as cloud hosting, data analysis, and SMS verification services.
  • Legal Obligations: We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in the good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with state laws, a court order, or a judicial proceeding.

5. Data Retention and Security

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. User Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information maintained by Speedo. You may update your profile information directly within the app. To request account deletion, please contact our support team. Upon deletion, we will remove your personal data, except for information we are required to retain by law or for legitimate business purposes.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
